Information We Collect

VetPathWay.Net collects practice information including name, address, and contact details. We gather user account data such as email, name, and credentials to provide our services. Patient and client records containing veterinary data are stored securely in our system. Billing and payment information is collected for subscription management. Usage analytics and system logs help us improve the platform and ensure optimal performance for all users.

How We Use Your Information

Your information enables us to provide and maintain VetPathWay.Net services effectively. We process payments and manage subscriptions to ensure uninterrupted access. Service updates and notifications keep you informed about important changes. Through analysis of usage patterns, we continuously improve our software and user experience. All processing adheres to HIPAA compliance standards and maintains the highest security protocols.

HIPAA Compliance

VetPathWay.Net is HIPAA compliant and maintains SOC 2 Type II certification.

We implement comprehensive administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect health information. All data undergoes encryption at rest using Transparent Data Encryption and in transit using TLS 1.3 protocols. Stringent access controls combined with detailed audit logs ensure data integrity and security across all operations.

Data Storage and Security

Our infrastructure leverages enterprise-grade Azure SQL Database with comprehensive security features. Multi-factor authentication adds an additional layer of protection, while regular security audits ensure ongoing compliance with industry standards.

Data is retained as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide services. After account closure, data is retained for seven years to comply with veterinary record-keeping requirements, then securely deleted using industry-standard data destruction methods.
